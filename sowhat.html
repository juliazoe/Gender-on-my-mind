WAGE INEQUALITY AND THE WORKPLACE

In the workplace, women need to work more days just to earn the same amount as men. This year, Equal Pay Day fell 83 days after the year started to represent how many more days women need to work to earn the same as men. Additionally, women are often prevented from getting promoted and moving into higher positions. Women are often targets of offensive statements and insenstivie questions.

SEXUAL ASSAULT AND HARRASSMENT

81% of women report that they have experienced some form of sexual harassment and/or assuault in their lifetime. Around 51% percent of femal victims of rape reported being raped by an intimate pratner, and 40.8% reported being raped by an acquainance. The estimated lifetime cost of rape is $122,461.
